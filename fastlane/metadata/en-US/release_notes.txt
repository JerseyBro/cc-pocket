What's new in 1.2.2

New
• See what a session changed: browse the files Claude touched and read each change, right from your phone
• One-tap new session: tap once and it starts under your default agent and mode — the chip beside it shows what you'll get and opens the full picker
• All your computers on one screen: online state, running projects and waiting approvals per machine, one tap to jump over
• LAN direct connect: on the same network the app talks straight to your computer for lower latency; removing a device now disconnects it immediately
• Links in replies are tappable, and lists refresh with a pull

Fixes & improvements
• A quick double-tap can no longer start two sessions
• Keyboard fixes around the composer; smoother model/mode switching
• Interrupted turns no longer snap context usage to a misleading 0%
• Resuming and observing desktop sessions is more reliable

Note: some features also need the computer-side cc-pocket-daemon on 1.2.2 — it now updates itself: run "cc-pocket-daemon update" (or brew upgrade / scoop update).
